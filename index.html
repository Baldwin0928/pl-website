Who We Are

Propulsive Landers @ Georgia Tech (GTPL) is a student-led team at the Georgia Institute of Technology dedicated to advancing propulsive landing technology. With a focus on hybrid propulsion and control systems, GTPL brings together a talented team of 100 students committed to advancing research and development of propulsive descent technologies.

Our Mission

In the coming future, we hope to accomplish our goal of becoming the first student team in the world to achieve hovering, vertical take-off, and landing (VTOL) of a hybrid rocket. GTPL is dedicated to providing students with hands-on experience and exposure to real-world engineering challenges beyond what is offered in class.

Vehicle Highlight

Hybrid Lander Prototype

Our current focus is a hybrid-propulsion lander capable of hovering and VTOL maneuvers. The vehicle combines a paraffin/N 2O engine, thrust vector control, and custom avionics to practice propulsive landing here at Georgia Tech.
